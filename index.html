AI-Powered Bead Art Designer

About

Ai Beads transforms your photos into beautiful bead art patterns using advanced AI technology. Design pixel-perfect Perler bead patterns with customizable board sizes, interactive 3D preview, and exportable blueprints.

Features

  • AI-powered photo to bead pattern conversion
  • Cutout mode for standees and keychains
  • Adjustable board size and color limits
  • Interactive 3D bead preview
  • Export PNG and PDF blueprints with color legend

FAQ

How are credits used?

Each AI generation costs 1 credit. New users receive 1 free credit upon registration. Additional credits can be purchased in the app or obtained via redeem codes.

Where is my data stored?

All your bead art creations are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store your images on any server.

Can I get a refund?

Credit purchases are handled through Apple's In-App Purchase system. For refund requests, please contact Apple Support directly.
