Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Introduction

Ai Beads ("we", "our", "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Authentication

We use "Sign in with Apple" for authentication. We only receive a unique user identifier and, if you choose to share it, your email address. We do not receive or store your Apple ID password.

Images

When you use the AI generation feature, your uploaded photo is sent to our server for processing. The image is used solely for generating the bead pattern and is not stored on our servers after processing is complete.

Local Data

All your bead art creations, gallery items, and settings are stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, phone number, or physical address
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties

In-App Purchases

Credit purchases are processed entirely through Apple's In-App Purchase system. We do not collect or store any payment information such as credit card numbers.

Data Security

All network communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS encryption. Your creations are stored locally on your device and are not accessible to us.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
